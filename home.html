About Our Church

The Seventh-day Adventist Church of LaFollette is part of the worldwide Seventh-day Adventist Church. Our church seeks to enhance the quality of life for people everywhere and to share the good news that Jesus is coming again soon.

We hope you will experience God's presence in our church and that you'll enjoy fellowship with our members and other visitors. We are all here to worship God and to develop a closer and more meaningful relationship with our personal Savior, Jesus Christ. We look forward to meeting you. May God bless you.
